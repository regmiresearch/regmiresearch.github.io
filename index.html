Welcome to the Regmi Research Group

Our research focuses on innovative solutions in agricultural and biological engineering, aiming to enhance productivity and sustainability in agricultural systems using ground-based sensors, UAV-based sensors, and remote sensing.

Research Areas

  • Agricultural automation and autonomy
  • Digital and Precision Agriculture
  • Sensing and Vision in Agriculture
  • Remote Sensing and GIS applications in Agriculture
