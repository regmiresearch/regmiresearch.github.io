About Us

In Regmi Research Group, we focus on leveraging deep learning, precision agriculture, and machine vision to modernize farming practices. Our research and teaching also involves Off-Road Vehicles, remote sensing, GIS, and UAV applications in agricultural technology. We are passionate about applying cutting-edge technology to create sustainable agricultural solutions and mentoring the next generation of engineers.
